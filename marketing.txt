SLIDE 3 — CORE FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"Let me walk you through what OpenDex actually does.

 REAL-TIME MARKET DATA
 The token list shows prices, 24-hour price change, market cap, trading
 volume, and holder counts — all refreshing automatically every two minutes.
 No stale numbers, no manual refreshes.

 DETAILED TOKEN PAGES
 Click any token and you get:
   - Current price with a live freshness indicator
   - 24H high and low, market cap, FDV, volume
   - Interactive OHLC price charts with multiple timeframes
     (5 minutes, 1 hour, 4 hours, 1 day, 1 week)
   - All active liquidity pools with TVL, fees, and trade volume
   - A direct 'Trade on Jupiter' button

 WATCHLIST
 Connect your wallet and pin any token to your personal watchlist for
 quick access. Watchlisted tokens are highlighted in the main table.

 COMMUNITY SENTIMENT
 Any wallet holder can cast a Bullish or Bearish vote on any token.
 The result is shown as a live sentiment bar on the token page and as
 a net score column in the main token list. Click your own vote again
 to remove it — no lock-in, no games."


SLIDE 4 — THE COMMUNITY SUBMISSION SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"Here's where OpenDex gets interesting — and where we're genuinely different
 from every other token explorer out there.

 Token communities can submit content for their token's page:
   - Custom banners
   - Social media links (Twitter, Telegram, Discord, websites)
   - Any other metadata the community wants displayed

 But — and this is the key — no single person decides what goes up.
 THE COMMUNITY DECIDES.

 Here's how it works:

 STEP 1 — SUBMIT
 You must hold the token in your connected wallet to submit content for it.
 This is verified on-chain via Solana RPC. No bots, no outsiders.

 STEP 2 — COMMUNITY REVIEW
 Submissions enter a review window. For a brand new token's very first
 submission, the window is just 5 minutes so tokens can get going quickly.
 For subsequent submissions — changes to existing content — the community
 gets a full hour to evaluate and vote.

 STEP 3 — WEIGHTED VOTING
 Other holders vote up or down. Votes are weighted by holdings:
   - 0.1% of supply = 1x weight  (minimum to participate)
   - 0.3%+ = 1.5x weight
   - 0.75%+ = 2x weight
   - 1.5%+ = 2.5x weight
   - 3%+ = 3x weight (maximum)

 This gives larger stakeholders more influence, while ensuring everyone
 with skin in the game has a voice. Whale-proof? No. Spam-proof? Yes.

 STEP 4 — AUTO APPROVAL
 When a submission reaches its approval threshold, it goes live automatically.
 No admin needed. The thresholds scale with market cap:
   - Under $50k market cap:  +10 weighted score
   - $50k–$100k:             +15
   - Over $100k:             +25
 Submissions that reach -10 are auto-rejected to quickly clear spam.

 STEP 5 — IT GOES LIVE
 The approved banner or link appears on the token's page for every visitor.
 The highest-voted content is displayed prominently. The community can keep
 voting to surface better content over time."

SLIDE 6 — TECH STACK (for technical audiences)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"Under the hood:

 FRONTEND
   - Vanilla JavaScript, HTML, CSS — no framework dependencies
   - No build step, no bundler. Loads fast, runs anywhere.
   - Hosted on Render as a static site

 BACKEND
   - Node.js with Express
   - PostgreSQL database on Render
   - RESTful API with rate limiting and input validation

 DATA SOURCES
   - Jupiter Aggregator — prices, metadata, holder counts
   - GeckoTerminal — OHLCV charts, liquidity pool data
   - Helius / Solana RPC — on-chain balance verification

 All wallet interactions are read-only. We verify holdings but never
 request transaction signing for votes or submissions. Your funds are safe."
